ABOUT US

Who are we

Welcome to J-Tours, a premier travel agency specializing in organizing tours to the Land of the Rising Sun. Our team of travel experts is passionate about sharing the beauty, culture, and history of Japan with our clients. We have been in the travel industry for over a decade and have established strong relationships with local tour operators, hotels, and restaurants to ensure that our tours offer the best possible value and experience.

Our Services

At J-Tours, we offer a range of tour packages to suit every budget and interest. Our tours range from week-long city packages to month-long exploration of Japan's four main islands. We can also customize tours to fit your specific needs and preferences. In addition to our tour packages, we also offer a range of travel services such as flight bookings, visa assistance, and travel insurance.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced travel professionals who are knowledgeable about Japan and are dedicated to providing the highest level of service to our clients. Our tour guides are all fluent in English and have a deep understanding of Japanese culture and history. They will go above and beyond to ensure that your trip to Japan is a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At J-Tours, we are committed to operating in a sustainable and responsible manner. We believe that it is our responsibility to preserve the natural and cultural heritage of Japan for future generations. We strive to minimize our environmental impact and support local communities through our tours. We also partner with responsible tourism organizations and follow their guidelines to ensure that our tours are sustainable and ethically-minded.
